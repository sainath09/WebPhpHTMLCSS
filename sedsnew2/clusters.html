The general body is divided into:-

1. EVENTS TEAM:

This will comprise of members who will organise the events such as general body meeting, guest lectures, workshops, debates, SEDS-SASTRA Conference etc.

2. OUTREACH TEAM:

This team consists of:-

a. Marketing team.

b. Media and Publicity

c. One Space Program

for school students and orphanages, where teams will be formed and assisted on a regular basis on various concepts of space science and possible mini-projects.

3. PROJECTS TEAM:

This team will consist of screened core members who are technically sound and involve themselves into projects of SEDS-SASTRA, sign M.o.U. with research organisations and companies for sponsorship etc. Each team will consist of an interviewed head chosen by the advisory committee in the presence of the President of SEDS-SASTRA. The club activities will be such that SEDS-SASTRA will be divided into teams with mix of members, as grouped by the executive committee, with heads and vice-heads for each team. Every club session shall involve healthy interactions and competitions between these club teams. BONUS points will be awarded to those team members who are active general body members. At the end of each year, there will be a SEDS-SASTRA Conference with reports, participation of schools that are part of ONE SPACE PROGRAM. The team with leading points shall be awarded the best team of the year. Also, active members shall be given certificates, allowance along with certificates for executive members.
