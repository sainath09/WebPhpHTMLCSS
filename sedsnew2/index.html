SEDS-SASTRA

Students for the exploration and development of space

Students for the Exploration and Development of Space, SASTRA chapter, was initiated under Strategic leadership of Praveen,N. It was inaugurated on 14/02/2013, with an auspicious note by Dr.V.K.Janardanan (Deputy Director, GSLV) under the guidance of Prof. M. Sridharan and Mr. M. Balasubramanian of SEEE and support from the management. It has turned into a vibrant team with a response from around 600 students with around 450, given membership, which will soon turn into one of the most successful space exploration and research teams in the world.

Objectives

1. SEDS strives to be an independent student-based organisation that encourages the exploration and development of space.

2. It encourages "discuss, learn about and implement" space technology.

3. To create future leaders who can aid the exploration and development of space.

4. To encourage members to understand the importance of space exploration and development.

5. To encourage members to build models/prototypes to better understand the working of space systems (rockets, satellites, space crafts, probes, rovers, orbiters etc).

6. To encourage members to explore space through ground and space based tools and instruments (telescopes, softwares, binoculars etc).

7. To encourage innovations in space science through paper presentations, research and development.

8. To encourage members to popularize space science in the country among students in particular and the public in general.

9. To encourage the development of lateral space related fields like space art, space music, space literature, space law etc. to augment the capabilities that space science enables.

10. To enable students to work in teams and share their knowledge as a means of personal growth and development.
